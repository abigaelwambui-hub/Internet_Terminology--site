Intranet

An intranet is a private network used within an organization, such as a company, school, or government office. It uses internet technologies (like web browsers and servers) but is restricted to authorized users only. Employees or students can access internal resources such as documents, applications, and communication tools through the intranet. Its main purpose is to improve collaboration, streamline workflows, and provide secure access to information without exposing it to the public.
